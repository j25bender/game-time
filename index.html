This game is broken into three levels.

  1. Break out of the atmostphere
  2. Break through space
  3. Break through a black hole to break out of this universe!

Instructions

  • Press Start to play
  • Move the mouse to move the paddle
  • Press the spacebar to launch the ball
  • Break bricks to score points!
